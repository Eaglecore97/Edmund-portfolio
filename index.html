Great design comes from curiosity, fundamentals, and adaptability — and AI is an extension of creativity, not a replacement.

I'm Edmund Chan, a graphic designer with six years across marketing agencies and in-house creative teams, partnering with diverse brands to pair strategic thinking with strong visual communication. My work spans social media design, packaging, branding & logo design, and AI art direction — always aiming for design that's purposeful, not just attractive.

A career highlight was creating visual assets for the Poker Dream International Event — large-scale work that sharpened how I perform under pressure and run one major project alongside two or three more at once. I also lead and mentor designers, guiding creative direction while keeping quality consistent across every output.

My toolkit is Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Premiere Pro, leaning Corporate, Minimalist, Luxury, and Modern. Lately I integrate Higgsfield, Seedance, Nano Banana, Kling AI, and ChatGPT to explore ideas faster and push more experimental, forward-thinking outcomes.

— Edmund
